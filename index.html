About

I’ve worked at Connect for more than 15 years. As a senior member of the team and developer I am responsible for all client and Connect owned online properties. As a designer I also work on print advertisements, magazines, tabloid style newspapers and campaign materials.

As part of my role I’m a key member of the organising team, managing ticketing, website, social media, e-newsletter and graphic production as well as logistics for the annual Scottish Dental Show.

IT support, management and decision making is a big part of my day-to-day work. From “have you turned it off and on again” calls, to large budget kit and infrastructure projects.

I love problem solving issues, making designs work for the user and looking for ways to make processes smoother and more efficient.
